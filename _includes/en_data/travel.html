Travel

Plane

If you're coming from the US, make sure you check the latest COVID testing requirements to enter France: https://www.interieur.gouv.fr/covid-19-international-travel. You'll need to show your vaccination records. Does your vaccination status count as "complete" according to French law? Message Alyssa/Paul-Henri if you have questions. If you're not sure, in general, it's the safest bet to get a PCR test before coming.

Car

If you're renting a car, make sure you pay attention to whether it's stick shift or automatic :) Also, please message Alyssa/Paul-Henri if you have extra space in your car and are willing to accept a car-pooler or two. Car is the easiest way to get around in the area of the venue - it's quite remote.

Train

There is a train from Paris to Nogent-le-rotrou, which is about 20 minutes from the venue. If you need help arranging a pick up from the train station, message Alyssa/Paul-Henri.

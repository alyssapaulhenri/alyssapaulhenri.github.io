How does the shuttle work?

You come yourself to the Domaine for the ceremony - there is a parking lot at the Domaine where you can leave your car. Then, throughout the night, as you're ready to go home, a woman who works at the Domaine will be there to shuttle you back when you like (even if it's at 4 am); in other words, there is not *one time* with *one shuttle* that departs. She'll wait with the minivan and you can ask her to bring you home (remember the name of where you're staying). You leave your car at the Domaine, and the same woman will come back and pick you up to come to the brunch in the morning.
